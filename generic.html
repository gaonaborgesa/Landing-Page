About Me

I grew up in Western NC, where I graduated with an Associate Degree before transferring to Berea College in KY, where I am now studying Computer and Information Science.

I love the cool mountain weather and enjoy listening to a variety of music in both English and Spanish. My goal is to learn a new natural language like Japanese soon. Outside of my academic and professional endeavors, I've been actively involved in community service, volunteering with the Hispanic Outreach Program, where I taught Spanish classes to adults.

I'm passionate about technology and its potential to solve real-world problems. I am eager to connect with professionals in the industry to explore potential career paths and opportunities for growth.
